Our Services

Services That We Offer For You

Collateral Management services:

These are designed to support trade finance credit transactions where our clients especially in the trading and agricultural sector, can secure short-term credit using their commodities as collateral security instead of the traditional fixed asset collateral-based lending.

Secured Distribution Services:

This service addresses the needs of the principal and agent relationships where the principal sends goods to the agent but still wishes to retain ownership of the goods. The goods are held in custody as the collateral until such a time when payment is received by the principal.

Monitoring and Inspection Services:

This is where the financier appoints VSM to specifically take charge of the consignment documentation, and monitor and physically inspect the goods from inland port to its final destination.

Warehouse Inspection services:

Here we provide ultimate protection to the parties of the contract e.g. Banks, clients, and ourselves by ascertaining that the proposed storage location is appropriate and safe for collateral custody.

Tax / Freight loan facility:

In instances where the importer has arranged payment terms with the supplier but cannot pay taxes and or freight after delivery, we arrange the Tax / Freight loan facility which enables the importer to meet these obligations on their imported goods.

Logistics business:

We offer specialized in-house training of middle-level and senior staff for commercial and development banks in trade finance, infrastructure setup, commodity finance structuring, agricultural credit management, business appraisal, and ongoing monitoring and evaluation of project performance.
