About Venture South Management

(VSM) is an integrated credit support service enterprise that was established to service the increasing need by business organizations to promote and simplify Trade Finance in the East African region. We specialize in offering Business Advisory, Trade Finance Support, and Transactional Risk Management solutions to our clients in the Agribusiness, Trade services, and the Finance sector.

The Company was registered in Uganda on 22 nd September 2022 with its Office located at Parliament Plaza on Parliamentary Avenue. VSM 's head Office is in Versoix, Switzerland and the Kenyan operational office is in Westlands, Nairobi.

Our expertise in credit support services relates to; Collateral management, secured distribution, monitoring, inspection, and field audits that have enabled local producers, exporters, importers, traders, and farmers' associations to access affordable credit finance through our partner Banks and Financial Institutions.
