About Us

We Help Our Clients To Grow Their Business

At DEMOS we use structured trading systems and advanced supply chain techniques to link our smallholder farmers to formal markets, credit and value addition opportunities including grain pre- cleaning and processing, bulking, packaging, and commodity exchange trading. The company which started its operations with the trading of locally sourced agricultural produce in Uganda has since expanded its operations to regional markets and corporate entities.

The mission of Demos Mercantile Ltd is to provide our customers and partners with seamless trading experiences, delivered by a team of professionals who are dedicated to promoting structured trading systems.

The vision of Demos Mercantile Ltd is to be the leading provider of quality grain using formal structured trading systems in Africa. Our expertise in commodity markets, supply chain management, trade finance and risk management place us in advantageous place to chieve this vision.
